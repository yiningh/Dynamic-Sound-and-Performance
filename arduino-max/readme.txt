======================

Using AArduino with two photocells and one potentiometer to control sounds in MAX/MSP.

Arduino setup:

one photocell connects to Arduino pin A0,
another to pin A1.

one pot connects to pin A4.

/////

in MAX/MSP, turn on the toggle.
use lights to change the readings of two photocells to change sound delays. 
turn the pot nob to change the sound speed.
